About me

Hi! My name is Ying Zhang (章颖). I’m a CS graduate student from ShanghaiTech University. Before that, I got my Bechalor’s degree from Communication University of China in 2022. My career goal is to become an excellent data scientist!

I hope to:

  • Analyze and optimize social system through data-driven methodologies
  • Develop advanced technological tools to facilitate daily life and increase productivity

Accordingly, my research interests primarily focus on data science and artificial intelligence, and their extensive applications in interdisciplinary fields like computational social sciences.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I enjoy exploring new techs🖥️, reading books📘, and making miniature models🏡 in my free time.

If you are interested in collaborating with me, feel free to get in touch! ✉️
